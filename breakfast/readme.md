# Tapioca

## Servings: 1 

## Time to prepare: 15 min

## Ingredients:
Tapioca
Cheese


## Equipment needed:
Pan or skillet

## Steps for preparation:
Put the tapioca in the pan and let it stir untill it glues together as a pancake
Cut the cheese in pieces
Put the cheese above the tapioca pacake 
Flipt it
